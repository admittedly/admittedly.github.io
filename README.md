Admittedly
====================
Admittedly is a seed-stage startup whose goal is to help students find and get into the right college. We use a JS stack, with Node.js on the backend and Backbone on the frontend. Our preferred database is Postgres, with a smattering of Redis when appropriate.

If you're interested in making one of the most stressful parts of high school a little less painful, check out [our jobs page](https://admitted.ly/jobs), or email [help@admitted.ly](mailto:help@admitted.ly).
